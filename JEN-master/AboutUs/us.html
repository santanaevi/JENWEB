ABOUT US


Thank you for using our website! We hope that it was very useful and helped you stay inspired and organized. Now we will explain a little bit about ourselves. Our names are Jiselle Ramirez, Evi Santana, and Niha Khanum (JEN).We all joined the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion program at KMPG in 2018. We were able to make this website by using what we learned about coding in the program. In today's society we use technology for everything whether it's for transportation, school, or work. The reason that we created this website is because as teenagers we are not the most organized, but as students organization is a must. It is also hard to stay motivated and everyone has those days where they don't want to get up. We wanted to make a website that is not only for teens but for anyone from young adults to professionals. This website is to help people stay organized and motivated.
